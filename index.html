Brief History of Nigeria

Independence

Nigeria achieved independence on October 1, 1960, through peaceful negotiations and political activism led by nationalist leaders like Nnamdi Azikiwe, Obafemi Awolowo, and Ahmadu Bello. A series of constitutional reforms and regional self-governance paved the way for a unified, sovereign nation.
